eProcurement
Management System

A secure, cloud-based bid and tender management platform purpose-built for South African government supply chain operations.

Platform Overview

eProcurement digitalises the full procurement lifecycle for government SCM units — from publishing tender notices and managing bidder queries to secure document storage and audit trail compliance. It replaces paper-based processes with a centralised system that promotes transparency, accountability, and efficiency.

Core Capabilities

Bid & Tender Management

Manage bids through Draft, Open, Closed, and Archived stages with category support for briefing registers, closing registers, and appointments.

Bidder Communication

Structured query threads between bidders and SCM officials with private/public visibility, read tracking, and email notifications.

Audit & Compliance

Full download audit trail with date filtering, CSV export, and a built-in SCM document library for compliance templates.

Bidder Self-Service Portal

Bidders register, verify via email, browse tenders, download documents, and submit queries without admin intervention.

Role-Based Access Control

Four roles with 14 capabilities ensure SCM managers, officials, unit managers, and bidders access only what they need.

Secure Document Storage

AES-256 encrypted cloud storage with MIME validation. Supports multiple providers with automatic local fallback.

User Roles & Permissions

Role Permissions
SCM Manager Full management — create, publish, close, archive bids; manage contacts, bidders, settings; view audit logs
SCM Official Operational duties — create and edit bids, respond to queries, upload documents
Unit Manager Read-only oversight — view bids, messages, and download logs
Bidder Self-service — browse tenders, download documents, submit queries, manage profile
